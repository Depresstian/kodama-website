Fermentation Recipe - Video I've found helpful

>1.Calculate the total weight of water + food
>2.Total weigtht * 0.025g = Grams of salt required
>3.Transfer liquid to new jar, add salt til weight reached
>4.Return to veggies, seal and store.

Pickling Recipe - Red Onion

>Bring equal parts water and 5% vinegar to a boil
>Add between 1-2tsp (or a "nice pinch") of pickling salt per cup of water-vinegar mix
>Boil, and add to jars filled with with cut veggies & optional aromatics.
>Make sure your jars won't shatter from the heat. Store in fridge
