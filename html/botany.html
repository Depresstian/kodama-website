MLE Agar Recipe & Process - 1/2 ratio is sufficient for 1 jar

Ingredients:
  • 500ml Distilled Water
  • 10g Agar-Agar Powder
  • 7.5g Light Malt Extract
Steps:
  • Boil Water
  • Mix dry components into water
  • Add to bottle and add food colouring
  • Steam bath for 90 minutes
