Buddhism - Non-orthodox Indian Philosophical School
Theravada buddhism: The establishment of the fundamental ideas at play. The buddha's direct teachings as primary resource
>Primary Texts: The Pali Canon (Tipitaka), the Sutta Pitaka: Digha Nikaya, Majjhima Nikaya, Samyutta Nikaya, Anguttara Nikaya

Mahayana buddhism: A further evolution of buddhism in some senses, generally keeps the framework but emphasis on new interesting ideas
>Texts: Heart Sutra
>Nagarjuna's Works ( Suhṛllekha: Letter to a Good Friend; Sunyatasaptati: 70 verses on Sunyata/emptiness; !hard Mūlamadhyamakakārikā: The Fundamental Wisdom of the Middle Way)
